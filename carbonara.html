Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Serves: 2

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Ingredients

50g parmesan

3 large eggs

200g spaghetti

sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

STEP 1

Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.

STEP 2

Break 3 egg yolks into a medium bowl, disgarding the whites

STEP 3

Grate 75g of parmesan

STEP 4

Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add 200g spaghetti and when the water comes back to the boil, cook at a constant simmer, for 10 minutes or until al dente (just cooked).

STEP 5

Mix the grated parmesan into the egg yolks and mix with a whisk over the steam from the pasta, taking it off the heat every so often so the eggs dont get cooked. Continue this until the mixture is smooth and and the pasta is ready

STEP 6

Drain off most of the water from the spaghetti, keeping a couple tablespoons of the pasta water in the pot. Then set aside the pasta to cool off for a minute

STEP 7

Mix in the parmesan and yolk mix into the spaghetti slowly and with care

STEP 8

Once combined use a long-pronged fork to twist the pasta on to the serving plate or bowl and grate a little more parmesan on top and season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

STEP 9

Use a long-pronged fork to twist the pasta on to the serving plate or bowl. Serve immediately with a little sprinkling of the remaining cheese and a grating of black pepper. If the dish does get a little dry before serving, splash in some more hot pasta water and the glossy sauciness will be revived.
